About Us

MultiAg Ltd is a three tier company, where Craig and Scott Wilson are co-owners and both have their own trading company. Craig Wilson’s trading company is Agricultural Spraying, while Scott Wilson’s trading company is Agricultural and Transport Engineering. Their co-owned company specialises in conventional square baling, big round baling/wrapping and selling bales.


The MultiAg boys grew up on a cropping, sheep and beef farm. They have always loved playing with machinery. At 19 years old they moved to Western Australia to work; for contractors, on farms, driving road trains, in iron ore mines and hard rock mines. After 5 years they came back to start up a hobby contracting business called MultiAg Limited.
