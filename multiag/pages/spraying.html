Spraying

Craig Wilson owns the Spraying part of MultiAg Ltd. The Sprayer is a 2016 V8 Toyota Land Cruiser. It has a 15m boom, 7.5m on each side. It has a 1200L stainless steel tank. The GPS is a John Deere Green Star SF3 running star fire. It has a 6000 receiver with 2cm accuracy, as well as a John Deere rate controller with sectional control and auto switching. This GPS records information for proof of placement.

All Agricultural Spraying can be done, from round up knockout, to fertilisers, pesticide, fungicide and more.
