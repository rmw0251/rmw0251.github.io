Baling

Last summer MultiAg Ltd owned a New Holland 548 wrapper baler and a John Deere baler. For this season they brought a McHale V660 high density variable chamber baler, which does the jobs of both of the older balers. This new McHale baler is good for grass, hay and straw balage.

They also own a New Holland high density conventional square baler. As well as a 10 pack accumulator attached to the end of the baler for easier stacking and carting.

There is an 8.5m wide twin spinner rake.

As well as a 3 point linkage fold around mower that gets attached to a tractor to mow the grass.
