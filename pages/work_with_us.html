COME WORK WITH US

Due to most of Meyer Contracting's work to be between October-March each year, we tend to mainly employ seasonal workers. A number of these seasonal workers come from Europe as they follow the grass around the world.

The tasks involved can include, silage carting, loader driving, pit covering, cultivating, raking, baling, effluent spreading and pond stirring.

You will Need:
  • A hard-working, positive attitude.
  • At least 2 years Tractor experience.
  • Full License with a Wheels Endorsement. Check out NZTA's website.
  • Additional experience with carting, balers, cultivators or loaders would be an advantage.
  • Overseas applicants will need either a work visa or a working holiday visa. Check out NZ immigration's website.
What we do for you:
  • Pick you up from Christchurch Airport when you land in New Zealand.
  • Provide a fully furnished and equipped rental for you and the other seasonal workers to live in. Includes, linen, towels, power and internet.
  • Provide Meyer Contracting work shirts.
  • Provide beverages courteous of our clients after a well finished job.
