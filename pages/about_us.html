ABOUT US

Established in the late 1970's, Alister Meyer Contracting started as a small baling business in Waimate. In the 2000's Alister sold out, only to buy it back in 2007 to form Meyer Contracting Ltd 2007.

Alister has a love for contracting and providing innovative and new solutions to local farmers which means that Meyer Contracting is one of the top contractors in South Canterbury that specialises in Silage Harvesting.

Quality is one of the greatest importances to Meyer Contracting and is why they have many loyal customers.
